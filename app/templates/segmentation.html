SEGMENTATION DYNAMICS
In memory management, SEGMENTATION is a technique that divides the memory into several portions or segments of different sizes. Unlike paging, segmentation provides a user-centric view of memory where processes are divided into logical units such as code, data, and stack.
Our simulator employs a Variable Partitioning model. As processes are loaded and removed, "holes" of free memory are created, leading to External Fragmentation.
